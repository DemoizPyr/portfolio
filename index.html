About Me

Behind the
lens.

I'm Harshith, a freelance photographer based in Hyderabad with a deep fascination for the interplay of light, shadow, and human emotion. My work spans editorial portraits, documentary landscapes, and intimate street photography.

Every frame I shoot is a conversation — between subject and environment, between stillness and motion, between what's visible and what's felt. I believe photography isn't about capturing what exists; it's about revealing what's always been there.

Available for editorial shoots, commercial projects, events, and personal commissions across India and internationally.
